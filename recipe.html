Lemmon Chicken

Try our award winning recipe for yourself.

Ingredients:

  • 14 oz. chicken breasts
  •   8 oz. chicken consomme
  •   2 tsp. garlic
  •   2 T. olive oil
  •   1 T. parsley, chopped
  •   1 T. capers
  •   2 T. butter
  •   1 lemon, halved

Instructions:

  1. Heat pan with olive oil. Sear chicken.
  2. Add garlic, lemon, and consomme to pan.
  3. Place in preheated 375F oven and bake 12-14 minutes.
  4. Remove chicken and lemon, add parsley and capers.
  5. Remove liquid in pan until 4 tablespoons remain.
  6. Remove pan from heat, wait 1 minute, and then add butter. Shake pan to blend.
  7. Pour sauce over chicken and serve.

Servings: 2
